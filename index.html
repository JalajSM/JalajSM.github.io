Introduction

About Me

I'm an engineer with a background in materials science, applied research, and computer science. At Columbia and Penn I made the Dean's List and spent most of my time in the lab. I've synthesized DNA crystals and imaged them under electron microscopes, built machine-learning models for electrolyte phase diagrams, and shipped products.

Along the way I interned at Northrop Grumman Mission Systems, held a fellowship at the National Energy Technology Laboratory, and now work on legal AI systems as a Product Special Projects Lead at August. My work sits between materials engineering and software, and the problems I like best are the messy interdisciplinary ones that don't have an obvious answer.

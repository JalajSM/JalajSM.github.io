Jalaj Sanjay Mehta

I'm an M.S.E. candidate at the University of Pennsylvania (graduating August 2026) and a Columbia University graduate with a B.S. in Materials Science & Engineering, where I made the Dean's List every eligible semester. My research has spanned DNA crystal metallization at Brookhaven National Lab, liquid electrolyte phase diagrams for next-generation batteries, and polymer flame-retardant hydrogels, including a publication in ACS Biomacromolecules. On the engineering and software side, I built transistor characterization tools at Northrop Grumman Mission Systems, wrote image-processing programs for the Department of Energy, and now work in legal AI as Product Special Projects Lead at August.
